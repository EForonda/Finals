STATEMENT OF MISSION

St. Louis School, Inc. (Center) is committed to:

  • PROVIDE QUALITY CHRISTIAN EDUCATION that is transformative to young boys and
    girls developing them to become: MAKA-DIYOS, MAKATAO,
    MAKABAYAN, MAKAKATARUNGAN,MAKAKASARIAN, MAKAKALIKASAN, so they can
    actively PARTICIPATE in BUILDING the family, community and the nation.
  • NURTURE a lifestyle that is simple and reverent of the earth and all creation promoting
    harmony through
    RIGHT RELATIONSHIPS of EQUALITY, PEACE, LOVE, FREEDOM and TRUTH;
  • INSTILL appreciation of and respect for our GENUINE CULTURAL HERITAGE, mindful of the INDIGENOUS PEOPLE’S CULTURE and their QUEST for SELF-DETERMINATION.
